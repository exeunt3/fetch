Personae is a worker-conscious commission market for conceptual personae ; agents of enunciation built from consented archives, available to surround already-running projects with the divergence automation cannot produce. After Deleuze & Guattari, What Is Philosophy? (1991) and A Thousand Plateaus (1980).

All personae shown are demo profiles. Prices reflect commissioned cognitive labor; payments are simulated.
